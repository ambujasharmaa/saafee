Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Saafe

What is Saafe and how does it work?

Saafe is a comprehensive financial data management platform that provides real-time aggregated views of your financial assets. It securely connects with multiple financial institutions, allowing you to manage and share your financial information based on your consent.

What financial institutions can I connect with?

Saafe integrates with various institutions including:

  • Banks & NBFCs
  • Mutual fund providers
  • Insurance companies
  • EPFO
  • Income tax portal
  • GSTN

How do I get started with Saafe?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Create your account
  2. Verify your phone number
  3. Connect your financial institutions
  4. Start managing your financial information

How does Saafe ensure the security of my data?

Saafe implements multiple layers of security:

  • End-to-end encryption with 256-bit base 64 encoded nonce
  • No Financial information stored
  • Secure, real-time data transmission
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks

What happens to my data when I share it?

Your data is shared securely and only with your explicit consent. You control what information is shared, with whom, and for how long. You can revoke access at any time, and all sharing is logged for transparency.

Can I control what information is shared?

Yes, you have complete control over your data sharing preferences. You can:

  • Choose specific accounts to share
  • Revoke access at any time

How often is my financial data updated?

Saafe provides real-time updates of your financial information. As soon as there's a change in your connected accounts, it's reflected in your Saafe dashboard immediately.
